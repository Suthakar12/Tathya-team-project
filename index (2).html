pBLAST (Protein BLAST) is a tool used to compare a protein sequence against a database of known protein sequences to identify similarities, evolutionary relationships, and functional insights. It helps in identifying homologous proteins, conserved domains, and potential functions of unknown proteins. Here we use the NCBI online BLAST tool to compare the provided protein sequence. Please enter the sample sequence in FASTA format in the box given and click "Run" for the result.
