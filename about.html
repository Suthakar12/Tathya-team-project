This website was developed as part of our final project in the Bioinformatics course, aiming to provide an efficient platform for comparative protein analysis. Our tool allows users to search for specific pathogenic proteins in bacterial datasets and perform BLAST-based comparisons to identify similarities and functional insights. Designed with researchers in mind, the platform simplifies protein sequence analysis, aiding in microbiology, genomics, and drug discovery studies.

This project was created by Tathya Oza, Suthakar, and Aswin Sunil, driven by our shared passion for bioinformatics and computational biology. We hope this tool serves as a valuable resource for researchers and students in the field.
